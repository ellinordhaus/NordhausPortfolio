VERA

VERA is a multi-cultural women’s interest group at the University of Minnesota. VERA proudly advocates for women's health and reproductive freedom by raising awareness, breaking stigmas, educating the public, and raising money for local women's clinics. I spent a year and a half as VERA’s Graphics Chair. I have also taken the role of PR Chair and I am currently VERA’s External Vice President. Here are some of the graphics, merchandise, and more I have designed as VERA’s Graphics Chair.

REPRODUCTIVE FREEDOM STICKERS

I designed these stickers in response to Roe v Wade being overturned. VERA sold these stickers and donated some of the proceeds to Red River Clinic, a local abortion clinic
